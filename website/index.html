About

Clash of Clans is a popular mobile strategy game developed by Supercell. Players build their own villages, train troops, and battle against millions of players worldwide. The game is known for its engaging clan system, epic wars, and regular updates that keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.

  • Build and upgrade your village
  • Join or create a clan
  • Compete in Clan Wars
  • Unlock powerful heroes and troops

Clans

Clans are the heart of Clash of Clans. By joining a clan, you can team up with friends or players from around the world to share troops, strategies, and participate in epic Clan Wars.

  • Donate and receive troops
  • Participate in Clan Games and Clan War Leagues
  • Earn rewards and climb the leaderboards

Town

Your Town is your base of operations. Upgrade your Town Hall to unlock new buildings, defenses, and features. Strategically place your defenses to protect your resources from enemy attacks.

  • Upgrade your Town Hall for new content
  • Defend your village with cannons, archer towers, and traps
  • Manage resources: Gold, Elixir, and Dark Elixir

Troops

Train a variety of troops to attack enemy villages and defend your own. Each troop has unique abilities and strengths, from the mighty Barbarian to the powerful Dragon and strategic Hog Rider.

  • Unlock new troops by upgrading your Barracks
  • Mix and match troops for the best attack strategies
  • Upgrade troops in the Laboratory for more power

Contact Us

Have questions or need support? Reach out to the Clash of Clans community or Supercell support for help.
